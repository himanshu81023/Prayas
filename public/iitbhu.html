Indian Institute of Technology (BHU)-Varanasi

Banaras Hindu University is one of the oldest and renowned college of India, was founded in 1919. Recently BHU is designated as an IIT in 2012. IIT Varanasi has 13 departments and three inter-disciplinary schools. Name of the Director is Dr.Pramod Kumar Jain.

Admission Criteria

  • B.Tech:Admission through IIT-JEE
  • For M.tech,P.hd,Research:Through GATE exam and IITJAM

Cource Offered

  • Ceramic Engineering,
  • Chemical Engineering,
  • Civil Engineering,
  • Computer Science and Engineering,
  • Electrical Engineering,
  • Electronics Engineering,
  • Mechanical Engineering,
  • Metallurgical Engineering
  • Mining Engineering
  • Industrial Engineering
  • Engineering Physics
  • Chemistry

Fee Structure

The course fees for B.TECH and 5 year Integrated courses is INR – 120000(Per semester) for Gen category  students and for SC/ST INR – 20000 (Per Semester).
