Kashi Institute Of Technology-Varanasi

Kashi Institute of Technology was established in the year 2008 under the aegis of Jain Educational Society. This institute is situated at Varanasi-Allahabad Highway. This institute is affiliated to UPTU, Lucknow and approved by AICTE, New Delhi.

Admission Criteria:

Candidates need to be passed their 10+2 Examination with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects along with minimum 45% (40% for SC /ST) Marks or  need to be qualified this exams:

  • UPSEE
  • JEE-MAINS

Courses Offered

  • Civil Engineering,
  • Computer Science and Engineering,
  • Electrical Engineering,
  • Electronics Engineering,
  • Mechanical Engineering,
  • Information Technology.
