Popular Hospital

Initiated and governed by Popular Medicare Limited, the hospital was started to provide general surgery and gynaecological services. However, soon it started widening its spheres to a number of medical faculties, and today it is one of the well known multispeciality centers in Varanasi. Housing an efficient team of doctors, supported by latest technical advents in medical domain, Popular Hospital is one name for effective and affordable health care
